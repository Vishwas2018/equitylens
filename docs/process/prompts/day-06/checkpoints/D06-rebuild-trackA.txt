    to fabricate published status to resolve rulesets in tests.

      If NODE_ENV === 'production' AND status !== 'published' AND
      ALLOW_DRAFT_RULESETS !== 'true' → throws Error mentioning 'production' and
      the offending status. Prevents unreviewed rulesets from reaching production
      silently. Draft resolution in test/dev is unrestricted.
